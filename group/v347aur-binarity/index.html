Hi all, I performed my BIS analysis on LSD profile using the JHK bands masks. To complete the discussion of last Thursday, I am computing the BIS as follows: ‚Ä¢ Ignore the first and last 15% of the profile (where the noise can strongly affect the automatic bissector computation). ‚Ä¢ Then I define the top and bottom regions as the top and bottom 25% of the line. ‚Ä¢ I measure the bissectors in 20 equally spaced vertical positions in each region, the final bissector of the region being the mean of these 20 measurements. ‚Ä¢ The BIS is then the bissector of the top region minus the bissector of the bottom region (anti correlated with an activity induced RV modulation).
